About Me

Hi, this is Dennis Lubrin here!

So, this page is supposed to give you, the reader, more information about who I am right? Well then, here goes...

I currently work as an IT Administrator, a RIS/PACS Administrator, unofficial HIPAA officer, and Web Developer/Website Manager for a radiology facility in New Jersey.

Ever since Middle School, maybe even before, I got interested in technology, specifically robotics... yeah... that didn't last very long.

But, that was the reason why I decided to go to a "special" High School called Middlesex County Academy for Mathematics, Science, and Engineering Technologies. Yes, it's a long name. I just call it The Academy.

Well, that was where I decided I don't like robotics, but I do like programming. So what do I take as my College major? Computer Science of course!

I went to Middlesex County College and got my A.A.S. degree in Computer Information Systems (ie. Programming). Unfortunately, they focused on programming using JAVA. The Web Development course, which I really, really, REALLY wanted to take, never happened. Why? Because either there's no professor, or there's not enough people who want to take it.

So, what do I do? Well, I take online courses to learn on my own. Courses, like the One Month HTML course!
